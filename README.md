The project shows the sales insights of the Atliq Hardware which sells millions of products each year. To track down the live sales and revenue/cost, I generated a PowerBI based report along with the dashboard to check what sales have patterns throughout the years and months.

The report helps to see:

1- Revenue per year 2- Revenue per month 3- Revenue per customer 4- Revenue per region 5- Top 5 customers 6- Top 5 sales Quantities 7- Sales by customer 8- Sales by city 9- Sales by year 10- Sales by month 11- Overall sales/Revenue

Tools that we used:

1- Microsoft Power BI Desktop 2- MYSQL Workbench
